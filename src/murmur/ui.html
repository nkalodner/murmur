Settings

connecting
Murmur is not running (or was quit). Start it in a terminal with murmur, then reload this page.
Hotkey
ctrl_r

Hold it to dictate; a quick tap locks hands-free. While changing, press the key you want. Esc stays reserved for canceling a recording. Bare keys like right Ctrl, right Alt, or F8 work best; a letter key would still type that letter everywhere.

Microphone

Applies to the next recording. The system default follows whatever the OS considers the default mic.

Dictionary
Vocabulary

Names and jargon, spelled and cased exactly how they should be typed. Transcripts that come out close snap to your spelling ("wisper" becomes "Wispr"). Stick to proper nouns; everyday words would get rewritten too.

Replacements

Exact fixes for things the model reliably mishears: whenever the left side shows up in a transcript, the right side is typed instead.

Match sensitivityHow close a word must sound before it snaps to your vocabulary. Left catches more, right is stricter. 0.82
